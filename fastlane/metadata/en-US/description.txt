ComeOnBack Pager is the facility console for the Come On Back ATC paging platform. Running on a shared workstation iPad, it gives the controller-in-charge a live picture of the operation — who is available, who is on position, and who has been paged — with one-tap paging to bring controllers back.

FEATURES

• Live facility board: available, on-position, and paged controllers at a glance
• One-tap pages with ASAP or timed be-back requests
• Canned messages for common instructions
• Training teams and planned position assignments
• Acknowledgements sync instantly with controllers' Come On Back apps

ComeOnBack Pager is for air traffic facilities using the Come On Back platform. Enrollment requires a facility console code issued by your facility administrator — the app is not usable without one.
